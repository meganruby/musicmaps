Music Maps


Discover the coolest music related sites in New York City.

Welcome

Welcome to Music Maps! If you're a music lover hoping to have a greater understanding of music in New York City or just someone looking for entertainment on your commute home, you've come to the right place. On Music Maps, we've marked some cool locations related to music in the city--from places like Tin Pan Alley, where popular late 19th century and early 20th century musicians such as Irving Berlin and Scott Joplin worked, to 1520 Sedgwick Avenue, known by many as the birthplace of Hip Hop

How It Works

Click on the Map button in the navigation bar, and you'll see a map with locations marked by musical notes, as well as your current location. If you click on each note, you'll be able to see some information on each location, including the address, a picture, a short summary, and a link to more information.
